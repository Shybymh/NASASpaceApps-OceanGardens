What is Ocean????

The Earth's surface is predominantly covered by the ocean, accounting for 70 percent of it. This vast body of water comprises
roughly 1.35 billion cubic kilometers (equivalent to 324 million cubic miles), representing approximately 97 percent of the total
water found on our planet. It is the ocean that sustains all life on Earth and gives our planet its distinctive blue appearance when
observed from outer space. Among the celestial bodies in our solar system, Earth stands alone as the only one confirmed to possess
liquid water.

The ocean is the largest reservoir of carbon in the world. Phytoplankton take part in photosynthesis and, thus, help with carbon sequestration. In turn, they release oxygen.
